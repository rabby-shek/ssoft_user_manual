New Loan Account Adding

Follow these steps to add a new loan account: -
First you need to successfully log in to the software.

Step-1
Click on the Loan Accounts section from the Control Panel.

Step-2
After clicking on the Loan Accounts section, a new list will appear from there

Step-3
Click on the New Loan Accounts section.

Clicking on the section will bring up a registration form.

We provide all the required information in the form accurately.

Step-4
After giving the information, click on the nominee information button.

Clicking on the Nominee Information option will bring up another new page.

All existing information has to be provided there.

Step-5
After providing the information, click on the Add Account button.
