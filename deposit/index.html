Add Deposit

Step-1
Click on the Deposit section from Accounting

Step-2
After clicking on the Deposit section, a new list will appear.
From there, click on the Add Deposit section.
Clicking on the section will bring up a form.
Provide all the information required in the form accurately.

Step-3
When the information is provided, click on the Deposit button.

Step-4
If you want to delete all the information given in the form, click on the undo button.
