Account Holders Information Update

Follow the steps below to update the account holder information:-

Step-1
First select the Account Holder option from Control Panel.

After clicking on the account holder option a new list will appear.

Step-2
From the list, click on the Update A / C Holder Info option.

After clicking on the A / C Holder Info option, a list of all the account holders can be seen.

Step-3
Click the Edit button to update a specific member's information.
