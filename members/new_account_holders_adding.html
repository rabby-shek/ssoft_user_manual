New Account Holders Adding

Follow these steps to add new members: -
You must first successfully log in to the software.

Step-1
From the Control panel, click on the Account holder section

Step-2
After clicking on the account holder section a new list will appear from there

Step-3
Click on Add A / C Holder section.

Clicking on the section will bring up a registration form. We provide all the required information in the form accurately.

Must provide (300 * 300) size picture in the registration form.

Step-4
After giving the information, click on the Accounts Information button.

Clicking on the Accounts Information option will bring up a new page.

All existing information has to be provided there.

Step-5
After giving the information, click on add member button.

New members can be added by following the above procedure.
