Update Loan Accont info

Follow the steps below to update the information in the loan account info: -
First you need to successfully login to the software.

Step-1
Select the Loan Account section from the Association.

A list will appear after clicking on the Loan Account section.

Step-2
Select the Update loan account Info option from the list.

All the loan account will be displayed after clicking on option.
Step-3
Click the Edit button to update the information on the specific account.

Step-4
If you want to delete the loan, click the Delete button.

A form will appear after clicking the Edit button.
Fill out the form correctly.

Step-5
Click the Update account button.
