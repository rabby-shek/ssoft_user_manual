Add New Savings Account

Follow these steps to add a new savings account: -
First you need to successfully log in to the software.

Step-1
Click on the Savings Account section from the Association.

Step-2
After clicking on the Savings Account section, a new list will appear from there

Click on the New Savings Account section.

Clicking on the section will bring up a registration form.provide all the required information in the form accurately.

Step-3
After giving the information, click on the nominee information button.

Clicking on the Nominee Information option will bring up another new page.

All existing information has to be provided there.

Step-4
After providing the information, click on the Add Account button.
