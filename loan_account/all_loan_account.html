All Loan Account

To view all All Loan accounts, follow these steps: -
First successfully login to the software.

Step-1
Click on the Loan Accounts section from the Association.

After clicking on the Loan Accounts section, a new list will appear from there

Step-2
Click on All Loans Accounts section.

Step-3
Click the Details button to view a specific member's information from the list.

After clicking on the Details button, all the information of the specified member will be displayed.

Step-4
Click on the History button to view the transaction history of specific members.
